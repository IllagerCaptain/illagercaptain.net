IllagerBot Privacy Policy

All data is kept in a private database, and shall only be stored for when it is needed.

Details:

  • Testers (Alpha and Beta) are stored to know whether they have access to experimental features and to keep the role on the official server if they left and rejoined.
  • Users that were temporarily banned are stored to know when they should be unbanned.
  • Data given in feedback, bug reports, and suggestions are only visible to developers.
  • Other arguments given to the bot (via commands, modals, etc) are cached temporarily before the interaction is replied to and then deleted immediently after.
