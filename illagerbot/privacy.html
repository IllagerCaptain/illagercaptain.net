IllagerBot Privacy Policy

All data is kept in a private database, and shall only be stored for when it is needed.

Details:

• The accounts of both alpha and beta testers are stored to know whether they have access to experimental features and to keep the role on the official server if they left and rejoined.
• To opt out, post it as a feedback request on the official server. Note that you will not be able to re-join this program.

• Data given in feedback, bug reports, and suggestions are collected and stored.
• To opt out of this, simply do not use this feature.
• If you are using this feature to opt out of any kind of data collection, it will be deleted after the opt-out is completed!
